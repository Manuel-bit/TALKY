Touch a Life Kenya Youth was born from the unshakable conviction that every human being has a priceless value and that each human being deserves a descent life free from ignorance, poverty and disease. All people of good will in the world endeavor to mitigate these three evils through their charitable giving. Touch a life Kenya Youth does not purport to eradicate these problems from all humanity; rather, it endeavors to address the problem of poverty among the youth. The Organization aims to address the situation that has condemned the young person(s) to a life of poverty be it educational, financial, social etc...

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR DR. TERESA WAMBUI

  See All

Help to for a better education in Kenya

Dr. Teresa Wambui is originally from Naivasha, Nakuru County. She is has been a lecturer of Philosophy at Chuka University for last twelve Years. After completing her Doctoral studies in Duquesne University in the United States she came back to Kenya driven by her urge to share her knowledge with Kenyan students. Dr. Wambui's academic journey is an amazing one. After completing her Primary education in 1974 that was class seven then, she was unable to proceed to High School for lack of school fees. She went for a tailoring training, courtesy of a well-wisher. It was not until 1982, that is, seven years after Primary School education did she get an opportunity to start her High School education, courtesy of Little Sisters of St. Francis. After completion of High School education she got a Scholarship to study in the United States, courtesy of Marywood University. This was followed by two more Scholarships from Duquesne University in the USA where she studied for MA in Systematic Theology and Ph.D in Philosophy....
